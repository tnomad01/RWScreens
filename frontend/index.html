Warrior Trading — Chart Rules & Workflow

Core Principles

  • Trade only in the direction of the trend: price above 9/20/200 EMAs = bullish bias.
  • VWAP is dynamic support/resistance on intraday charts.
  • Favor pullbacks to key levels (9 EMA, 20 EMA, VWAP, +1 SD band) for entries.
  • Risk: tight stops below VWAP or recent low; target 2:1+ or 10%+ moves.

Chart Ordering — Pre-Entry

  • ① Daily Context → confirm price above 200 EMA.
  • ② Intraday Momentum → confirm price above VWAP + 9/20 EMAs + catalyst.
  • ③ Scalping Fast → check for tightening setup.

Chart Ordering — Entry

  • Primary decision: Intraday Momentum — pullback to VWAP/9 EMA then reclaim.
  • Timing: Scalping Fast + Micro Scalping — first new high or micro pullback break.
  • Final confirmation: all three faster charts aligned with volume spike.

Scenario A — "Looking Good" ✓

Daily above 200 EMA → Intraday pulls to VWAP/9 EMA, reclaims with volume → Fast/Micro show first-candle new high on high volume → price walks up 9 EMA.

Scenario B — "Getting Stopped" ✗

Daily near 200 EMA resistance → Intraday VWAP reclaim immediately fails → Fast charts show 9 EMA loss + red volume spike, no new highs → quick stop.

Scenario C — Mid-Trade Warning ⚠️

Intraday still above VWAP but hugging 9 EMA. Fast/Micro show multiple failures to make new highs + volume drying up → tighten stop or exit on first 9 EMA loss.
